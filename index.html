Who We Are

A Native Engineering Firm Built for Impact

JOBIRA Engineering PLC provides design, planning, sustainability consulting, digital solutions, and project management for buildings, cities, transportation, civil infrastructure, water, gold mining, and the environment.

Founded in 2017 GC, we've grown from a small team into a professional community of engineers, architects, planners, economists, sustainability specialists, and multidisciplinary experts — all united by one mission.
